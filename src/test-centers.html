Test Centres

What is a Postal Test?

This is a system where individuals are allowed to purchase their mandatory COVID 19 tests kits from authorized providers and the kits are sent to them by courier. The individuals will take their own samples or conduct the tests at home and transfer the swabs or results to their laboratories by mail post for certificate release.

At Travel Test Global, we have set-up an inter-country network of Laboratories through which our customers can have seamless pick-up and courier of COVID 19 test kits to their desired house addresses while results are released within 24 to 48 hours according to the country's regulations.

What is a Walk-In Test?

Inviduals can get their COVID 19 tests by healthcare workers, by visiting approved Laboratories, Clinics, Health Centers, Hospitals or Pharmacies.

At Travel Test Global, we have a network of Walk-In testing centers where our customers can take their tests on appointment basis. Results are released within 24 to 48 hours according to the country's regulations.

What is a Home Test?

If you prefer a COVID 19 test that is supervised or conducted by a healthcare worker in your house, then we have got you covered. Home tests are suitable for individuals who are unable to visit the clinic or conduct the tests on their own due to their health conditions or observing self-isolation.

At Travel Test Global, we have a network of Laboratories, Health Centers and Clinics that can deploy healthworkers to conduct home tests on appointment basis in major cities around the world. Results are released within 24 to 48 hours according to the country's regulations.

What is a Drive-Through Test?

Drive-Through testing centers operate just like fast-food restaurant drive-throughs. They are designed to address social distancing and crowd control at COVDI 19 testing centers and they have been adopted by several countries.

At Travel Test Global, we have partnered with a network of Laboratories, Health Centers and Clinics that operate 24 hour drive-through centers in major cties globally. Results are released within 24 to 48 hours according to the country's regulations.
